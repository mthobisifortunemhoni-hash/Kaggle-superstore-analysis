# Kaggle-superstore-analysis
##. Objective
To evaluate sales performance across different regions and identify key trends.

##. Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel.
- Pivot Tables.
- Charts.


##. Key Findings
- The Central region recorded the highest total sales.
- Some regions performed significantly lower than others.

##. Insights
- Sales performance varies across regions
- This may indicate differences in demand or market conditions (not confirmed in dataset). 

##. Recommendations
- Further analysis is needed to understand why some regions underperform.
- Focus marketing and sales strategies on high-performing regions.
- Investigate opportunities to improve low-performing regions.
